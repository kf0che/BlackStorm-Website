Business Portal Features

Built for MSP Operations

Every feature in the Business Portal is designed around the workflow of a technician team managing multiple client environments simultaneously.

Multi-Client Dashboard

See every client's status at a glance — uptime, active alerts, device count, and overall health — in a single consolidated operations view.

Cross-Client Alert Management

Acknowledge, track, and close alerts across all client environments from one screen. Never lose an open alert because it's buried in a per-client view.

Role-Based Access Control

Assign technicians appropriate access levels. Tier-1 staff see what they need; senior engineers have broader access. No over-permissioning by default.

Per-Client Drill-Down

Click into any client environment for a full detailed view — device list, alert history, performance charts, and reporting — without switching tools.

Fleet-Level Reporting

Generate reports across all clients simultaneously or individually — monthly performance summaries, uptime attainment, and incident logs ready to send.

Real-Time Operations Feed

A live activity feed showing alert events, recovery notifications, and device status changes across all monitored environments in real time.

Use Cases

Who Uses the Business Portal

Managed Service Providers

MSPs managing 5 to 500+ client environments need a centralized operations view. The Business Portal is designed specifically for MSP technician workflows — fast, consolidated, and organized by client.

  • All clients in one view
  • Technician role management
  • Per-client Uptime reporting

Multi-Site Businesses

Businesses operating across multiple locations — offices, warehouses, branches — need visibility into every site from a single operations team. The portal aggregates all sites into one operational view.

  • Multi-location visibility
  • Site-level drill-down
  • Consolidated alert management

Operations & NOC Teams

Network Operations Center teams need an continuous, real-time view of the entire monitored estate. The Business Portal's live feed and consolidated alert view is built for continuous operations monitoring.

  • Live activity feed
  • Real-time alert status
  • Audit-ready incident logging
