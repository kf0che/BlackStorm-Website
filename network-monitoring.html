What BlackStorm Monitors

Our monitoring platform covers the full range of IP-addressable devices in your network environment — from core infrastructure to edge devices and servers.

Servers & Hosts

Physical and virtual servers, cloud instances, and application hosts. Monitor availability, response time, and basic service health.

Routers & Switches

Core network equipment including routers, managed switches, and backbone infrastructure — the devices your entire network depends on.

Firewalls & Security Appliances

Network security devices including firewalls, UTM appliances, and VPN gateways. Know immediately if your perimeter goes offline.

Wireless Access Points

Monitor your wireless infrastructure for availability — ensuring your Wi-Fi endpoints are online and reachable across your facility.

Network-Attached Storage

NAS devices and storage systems that your team depends on for file access, backups, and data operations — monitored for continuous availability.

Custom IP Devices

Any IP-addressable device in your environment — from industrial controllers to smart facility equipment — can be added to your monitoring profile.

How It Works

From Onboarding to Full Visibility

Getting your environment monitored is a fast, guided process. Most clients are fully operational within a few business days.

1

Environment Assessment

We work with you to map your network, identify critical devices, and understand your operational requirements and alert preferences.

2

Configuration & Baseline

Monitoring is configured for your specific environment. Baselines are established so alerts are meaningful — not noisy false positives.

3

Continuous Monitoring

Polling begins. Your devices are checked continuously. Performance data accumulates and dashboards populate in real time.

4

Alert & Respond

When a device goes down or a threshold is breached, notifications go out immediately. You have the data you need to respond — fast.

Alerting

Be the First to Know. Every Time.

When a monitored device goes offline, exceeds a latency threshold, or shows abnormal behavior — BlackStorm sends a notification through your preferred channel.

You configure what matters and how you want to be notified, with delivery depending on your plan and notification channel.

  • Email alerts — immediate notifications to one or more addresses
  • SMS alerts — text message notifications for urgent issues (Professional and above)
  • Dashboard alerts — visible in your customer or business portal in real time
  • Alert escalation — configure escalation paths if primary contacts are unreachable
  • Recovery notifications — know when a device comes back online, automatically
