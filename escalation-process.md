# Escalation Process

## Purpose

Escalation helps BlackStorm, LLC move important issues to the right level of attention without creating unnecessary alarm.

Escalation should be based on business impact, operational risk, available context, and customer communication needs.

## Escalation Triggers

### Business Impact

The issue affects a key workflow, location, or customer operation.

### Monitoring or Visibility Impact

The issue affects the ability to monitor a customer environment or understand current device/service health.

### Repeated or Worsening Issues

The same issue repeats, expands, or shows signs of worsening.

### Sensitive Communication Needs

The issue requires careful customer updates, leadership visibility, or coordination with a third-party provider.

## Escalation Practices

- Confirm the current facts before escalating.
- Keep customer-facing language calm and factual.
- Document what changed and why priority changed.
- Do not imply guaranteed response or resolution times.
