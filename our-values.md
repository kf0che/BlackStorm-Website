# Our Values

BlackStorm, LLC was built around a simple idea: technology services should be honest, useful, and understandable. Network monitoring should help business owners, MSPs, and technical teams see what is happening in their environments without confusing language or inflated promises.

These values are not marketing language. They are how we make decisions about services, pricing, support, security, and future growth.

## We Tell the Truth

BlackStorm is a growing company, and we are clear about that. We do not exaggerate our size, overstate our capabilities, or promise services we have not built yet.

When something is available, we say so. When something is planned or still being researched, we label it clearly. When we make a mistake, we work to own it and correct it.

Customers should always understand what they are getting from BlackStorm and what they are not.

## Your Privacy Is Not for Sale

We do not sell customer information. We do not share monitoring data with advertisers. The network monitoring data we collect about your devices belongs to you.

We use customer information to provide the services customers sign up for, including monitoring, alerts, dashboards, reports, account support, and billing. We do not treat customer data as a product.

## Technology Should Be Understandable

Many business owners need reliable technology but do not want technical jargon. MSPs and technical clients may want deeper detail, but they still benefit from clear explanations.

BlackStorm writes and designs for both audiences. Our goal is to make network monitoring understandable to a non-technical business owner while still being credible to technicians, MSPs, investors, and security-conscious clients.

## Reliability Matters

When customers trust us to monitor their network, we take that responsibility seriously. Our Network Monitoring Service is designed to help customers see when important devices are online, offline, slow, or showing signs that need attention.

No technology service can promise perfection. We do not claim that outages, missed alerts, or service interruptions can never happen. We do commit to building, maintaining, and improving the service with care.

## Every Customer Matters

BlackStorm serves small businesses, growing companies, MSPs, local organizations, and teams that need better visibility into their network environments.

Every customer deserves respectful communication, clear expectations, and useful service regardless of plan size or technical background.

## We Build Before We Announce

We only offer services that are actually available. The Network Monitoring Service is the current core BlackStorm service because it is the service we are building around.

Future initiatives may include improvements to reporting, dashboards, alerting, MSP workflows, and security-aware visibility. Any future initiative should be clearly marked as planned, researched, or unavailable until it is ready.

BlackStorm will not accept payment for services that do not exist, and we will not publish timelines we cannot responsibly support.

## Integrity in Practice

BlackStorm is committed to operating with honesty and transparency. That means:

- No inflated claims
- No hidden fees
- No fabricated credentials
- No unsupported compliance claims
- No claims of certifications that have not been earned
- No claims of customer relationships or market reach unless they are accurate and documented
- No selling customer data
- No pressure tactics

Trust is built through consistent, honest action over time. We are proud of what we are building, clear about what is still developing, and committed to growing in a way customers can trust.

## Who We Serve

BlackStorm is for organizations that need practical visibility into their technology environment.

### Small Businesses

Know when key network devices or services stop responding before the issue turns into a customer-facing problem.

### MSPs

Give technicians and clients clearer visibility into monitored environments, alerts, and reporting.

### Growing Companies

Start with essential monitoring and expand coverage as locations, devices, and operational needs grow.

### Local Organizations

Use plain-language network visibility without needing a dedicated internal IT department.

## We Want Customers to Understand What They Are Buying

Network monitoring is not a complicated idea, but the industry often makes it sound complicated. BlackStorm explains services plainly so customers can make informed decisions.

If something on the website, in a service description, or in support documentation is unclear, we want to improve it.
