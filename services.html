Flagship Service

Network Monitoring Service

Continuous, 24/7 monitoring of your critical network infrastructure. BlackStorm tracks uptime, latency, packet loss, and device health — alerting you when something deviates from normal.

Whether you're protecting a single office network or managing hundreds of client sites as an MSP, our monitoring platform delivers the visibility you need to act before customers are impacted.

  • Routers, switches, firewalls, servers, and custom devices
  • Configurable polling intervals for fast detection
  • Multi-channel alerts: email, SMS, dashboard
  • Historical trend data and performance reports

Security Service

Security & Compliance

BlackStorm approaches infrastructure design and operations with security as a foundational layer — not a feature bolted on afterward. We help clients understand their security posture and move toward a more defensible, audit-ready operational state.

We do not claim compliance certifications for clients. Instead, we help position businesses with the documentation, visibility, and operational practices that make compliance conversations — with clients, auditors, or leadership — more credible.
