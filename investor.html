Market Opportunity

Every Business Has a Network. Few Have Visibility.

The network monitoring and visibility market is large and growing — driven by digital transformation, remote work infrastructure, increasing cybersecurity awareness, and regulatory pressure across every industry sector.

Yet the majority of small and mid-sized businesses — and even many MSPs — operate without professional, continuous monitoring. They rely on reactive discovery (customers calling about outages) rather than proactive detection. BlackStorm exists to serve this underserved market segment with a professional, accessible service.

  • Millions of SMBs operating without professional monitoring
  • Growing MSP market with demand for white-labeled portal solutions
  • Increasing regulatory and compliance pressure requiring documented visibility
  • Security incidents creating urgency for proactive monitoring investment

Business Model

Recurring Revenue. Scalable Delivery.

BlackStorm operates on a subscription-based model — providing predictable recurring revenue and low churn characteristics typical of mission-critical infrastructure services.

Subscription-Based MRR

Monthly recurring revenue from Starter, Professional, Business, and Enterprise plan subscriptions. Infrastructure monitoring is a "sticky" service — clients rarely cancel once their environment is actively monitored and they're accustomed to the visibility it provides.

MSP Channel Strategy

MSPs represent a high-value customer segment — a single MSP client can represent dozens of end-client environments. BlackStorm's Business and Enterprise plans are designed to serve MSP workflows, creating significant per-client revenue at scale.

Platform Expansion Revenue

Future BlackStorm services — all designed to integrate with the existing platform — create natural upsell opportunities and expand revenue per customer without proportional acquisition cost increases.

Investment Thesis

Why BlackStorm

Essential Service Category

Network monitoring is not a discretionary IT expense — it is an operational necessity. Businesses that adopt professional monitoring rarely abandon it. Churn risk is structurally low for mission-critical visibility tools.

Platform, Not Product

BlackStorm is building a platform that expands in value over time. Each new BlackStorm service integrates with the existing infrastructure — creating a compound data and service moat as the product suite grows.

MSP Channel Multiplier

A single MSP customer can represent 5–100+ underlying client environments. MSP-oriented pricing and portal tools create significant per-customer revenue leverage that is difficult to replicate with direct-SMB-only models.

Security Demand Tailwind

Cybersecurity awareness, regulatory expansion, and increasing cyber incidents are driving demand for security-adjacent services. BlackStorm's compliance-aware positioning aligns with this sustained demand trend.

Transparent Operations

BlackStorm operates with a commitment to transparency — in client communications, compliance positioning, and business reporting. We don't overstate our capabilities or misrepresent our maturity.

US-Based Operations

BlackStorm is a US-based LLC with US-based operations, support, and data handling practices — addressing growing client and regulatory preferences for domestic technology service providers.
