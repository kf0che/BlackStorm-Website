# Ticketing Workflow

## Overview

The ticketing workflow should help BlackStorm, LLC receive, organize, and communicate support requests without overwhelming customers or internal operators.

## Workflow Stages

### 1. Submitted

The customer shares the issue, affected service or device, and contact information.

### 2. Triage

BlackStorm reviews the request, checks available monitoring context, and assigns an initial priority.

### 3. In Review

The issue is being investigated or queued for follow-up.

### 4. Waiting on Customer

Additional details, access, confirmation, or scheduling may be needed from the customer.

### 5. Planned Follow-Up

Work has been identified and is waiting for the appropriate next action.

### 6. Resolved or Closed

The issue has been addressed, documented, or closed with a clear reason.

## Workflow Notes

- Do not hardcode future SLA promises into public pages.
- Avoid implying guaranteed resolution times.
- Keep internal notes separate from customer-facing updates.
