# BlackStorm Services

BlackStorm, LLC provides practical network monitoring and related visibility services for small businesses, MSPs, and growing organizations.

Our services are designed to help customers understand what is happening in their network environment without overwhelming them with jargon.

## Network Monitoring Service

The BlackStorm Network Monitoring Service is the core service offering.

It helps customers track whether important network devices are online, reachable, and responding within expected ranges. When something changes, customers can receive alerts and review status information through reports or dashboards.

### What Can Be Monitored

Depending on the customer's environment and plan, monitored devices may include:

- Routers
- Switches
- Firewalls
- Servers
- Network-attached storage
- Wireless access points
- Other IP-addressable devices approved by the customer

### What Monitoring Can Show

Network monitoring can help show:

- Device availability
- Response time
- Latency trends
- Outage history
- Recovery events
- Basic uptime reporting
- Alert history

### What Monitoring Does Not Guarantee

Network monitoring does not guarantee that a device will never fail, that an alert will never be delayed, or that all service issues will be detected.

Monitoring results reflect what BlackStorm's systems are able to observe from configured monitoring methods and locations.
