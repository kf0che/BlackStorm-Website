About BlackStorm, LLC

Built on Integrity.
Focused on Security.

We believe every business deserves professional infrastructure visibility and security-focused technology services — regardless of size or technical depth.

Our Mission

Operational Clarity for Every Business

BlackStorm, LLC was founded on a straightforward principle: businesses deserve to know what's happening in their technology environments at all times. Too many organizations operate with critical blind spots — unmonitored servers, unchecked uptime, and no visibility into whether their infrastructure is performing or failing.

We exist to change that. By delivering professional monitoring, security-focused design, and intuitive customer portals, BlackStorm gives businesses the operational clarity they need to serve their customers confidently and meet the demands of modern technology environments.

Every service we offer is built with the end user in mind — whether that's a business owner reviewing their uptime report, a network technician responding to an alert, or an investor evaluating infrastructure maturity.

Security-focused from day one. Every design decision starts with a security mindset — access controls, data handling, and threat awareness built into the foundation.
Compliance-aware by design. Our processes align with NIST Framework and CISA guidance, positioning clients for audit-ready operations without overpromising certifications.

Core Values

What We Stand For

Our values aren't aspirational posters on a wall. They're the operating principles behind every service, every decision, and every client interaction.

Security First

Every service we design begins with a security mindset. We consider threats, access controls, and data protection at every layer — before deployment, not after an incident.

Radical Transparency

We don't hide behind jargon or vague promises. Our portals, reports, and communications are clear, honest, and built for business owners — not just engineers.

Operational Reliability

We build for uptime — ours and yours. Our monitoring infrastructure is engineered to remain operational when systems around it fail, because your visibility matters most in a crisis.

Customer Partnership

Our clients aren't ticket numbers. We build long-term relationships and design services that grow as your business grows — not plans that force you into unnecessary upgrades.

Our Approach

Security-Aware. Compliance-Conscious.

BlackStorm operates with a security-first philosophy aligned with guidance from NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) and CISA (Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency).

While we are not a compliance certification body and do not claim regulatory certifications on behalf of our clients, we design our processes and systems with compliance-awareness as a foundational principle — not an afterthought.

This means our monitoring, data handling, and operational practices are built with audit-readiness in mind. When regulatory or client compliance requirements arise, businesses using BlackStorm are starting from a stronger, more defensible position.

  • Designed with NIST Cybersecurity Framework in mind
  • Aligned with CISA security best practices
  • Audit-ready operational visibility
  • Transparent data handling practices

NIST Framework Aligned

Our service design follows the Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover framework — the industry-standard model for cybersecurity posture.

CISA Guidance Informed

We monitor CISA advisories and incorporate relevant guidance into our operational practices and customer recommendations.

Audit-Ready Visibility

Historical monitoring data, event logs, and performance reports provide a documented record of your infrastructure's operational state.
