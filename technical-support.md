## What Support May Need From You

To help quickly, include:

- Your name
- Business or organization name
- Best contact email or phone number
- Device name or IP address, if relevant
- Description of the issue
- When the issue started
- Screenshot or report reference, if available
- Whether the issue is urgent

Do not send passwords through email or contact forms.

## Monitoring Support Boundaries

BlackStorm monitoring can show that a device appears offline, slow, or unreachable from the monitoring system.

Monitoring support does not automatically include:

- On-site repair
- Managed IT support
- Hardware replacement
- Vendor warranty work
- Legal or compliance advice
- Full security incident response

If BlackStorm offers additional support options in the future, those options should be described clearly in the relevant plan or written agreement.

## Alert Questions

### Why Did I Receive an Alert?

An alert usually means a monitored device did not respond as expected or crossed a configured threshold.

Possible reasons include:

- Device outage
- Internet service issue
- Power loss
- Firewall or routing change
- Temporary latency
- Monitoring configuration issue

### Why Did the Device Look Fine When I Checked?

Some outages are brief. A recovery may happen before someone manually checks the device. Reports and alert history can help show the timeline.
