Portal Features

Everything a Client Needs. Nothing They Don't.

The Customer Portal is designed for the business owner or IT manager who needs to understand their network status without needing to interpret raw technical data.

Real-Time Status

Device status, uptime percentage, and current latency — updated continuously and displayed in plain language anyone can understand.

Alert History

A clear log of all alerts and recoveries — when a device went down, how long it was offline, and when it came back. No ambiguity.

Performance Reports

Monthly performance summaries with uptime percentages, incident counts, and uptime attainment — ready to download and share.

Device Inventory

A clear list of all monitored devices in the client environment — with current status, labels, and location context where configured.

Secure Access

Clients authenticate with their own credentials. Access is scoped to their environment only — no cross-client data exposure.

Uptime Documentation

Uptime attainment documentation — useful for clients who need to demonstrate infrastructure reliability to their own stakeholders.

The Value of Visibility

Why Client Visibility Builds Better Relationships

When your clients can see the same uptime data and alert history you see, something important changes: they stop wondering and start trusting.

Clients who have portal access call less — not because they don't care, but because they already know. When an incident does occur, they've seen the alert notification, they've seen the recovery notification, and they understand what happened. That's a fundamentally better support relationship.

For MSPs, the Customer Portal is a differentiator. It communicates professionalism, transparency, and operational maturity to every client who logs in.

  • Reduces "what's the status of my network?" calls
  • Demonstrates professionalism to clients and executives
  • Creates a paper trail of uptime and incident history
  • Differentiates your MSP from competitors who don't offer this
