# Priority Levels

## Purpose

Priority levels help BlackStorm, LLC sort support requests by operational impact. They should guide triage without promising fixed response or resolution times.

## Suggested Priority Labels

### Low

General questions, documentation updates, minor requests, or non-urgent improvements.

### Normal

Issues that affect a workflow but have a reasonable workaround.

### High

Issues that significantly affect business operations, monitoring visibility, or important customer workflows.

### Urgent

Issues that may involve active service disruption, broad business impact, or time-sensitive operational risk.

## Important Notes

- Priority may change as more information becomes available.
- Priority is not the same as guaranteed response time.
- Customers should be encouraged to describe impact clearly.
- Security-sensitive concerns should be handled carefully and without asking for passwords or secrets.
