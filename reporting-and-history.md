# Reporting and History

## Overview

Reporting gives customers a clear record of monitoring activity, support trends, and operational changes over time.

Reports should be useful for business conversations without overstating what the platform can verify.

## Report Types

### Health Summary

High-level service and device status for a selected time period.

### Alert Summary

Alert volume, grouped alert types, and current follow-up status.

### Incident Summary

Incident count, current state, major updates, and resolution notes.

### Uptime and Availability

Uptime statistics when reliable data sources are available.

## History Principles

- Preserve timestamps and status changes.
- Distinguish monitored data from support notes.
- Avoid unsupported uptime or compliance claims.
- Make export and review workflows clear.
