# Monitoring Philosophy

## Purpose

BlackStorm, LLC approaches monitoring as a way to help customers understand what is happening in their environment without overwhelming them with raw technical noise.

The monitoring experience should answer three practical questions:

1. What needs attention?
2. What changed recently?
3. What should happen next?

## Customer Trust

Monitoring should build confidence through clear language, visible history, and honest status labels. It should not imply that every issue can be prevented or that every environment is fully protected.

## Operational Clarity

The platform should separate urgent issues from routine notices. Customers should be able to tell the difference between:

- Healthy services
- Items that need review
- Planned maintenance
- Active incidents
- Historical reports

## Alert Fatigue Reduction

Alerts should be grouped, prioritized, and explained in plain language. Repeated low-value notifications should be reduced where possible.
