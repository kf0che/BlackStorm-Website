# Operational Visibility

## Overview

Operational visibility gives customers and support teams a shared view of service health, device condition, alerts, incidents, and recent activity.

The goal is not to create a noisy control room. The goal is to make operational status easy to scan and easy to explain.

## Core Visibility Areas

### Service Health

Show whether monitored services appear healthy, need review, are paused, or are unavailable.

### Device Health

Summarize device status in customer-friendly terms. Avoid assuming that every device issue is a security incident.

### Alert Activity

Group alerts by importance, source, and current state.

### Incident Context

Show what happened, when it changed, and what the next expected action is.

### Reporting History

Provide enough history to support business conversations, support reviews, and planning.
