# Support Philosophy

## Purpose

BlackStorm, LLC support should feel calm, clear, and accountable. Customers should understand how to ask for help, what information is useful, and what will happen next.

Support language should be professional without making promises the business is not ready to guarantee.

## Trust Principles

- Use plain language.
- Ask only for information needed to understand the issue.
- Do not ask customers to share passwords or secrets.
- Confirm receipt and next steps when integrations are available.
- Separate urgent issues from routine questions without promising fixed response times.

## Customer Experience Goals

### Make Support Easy to Start

The first step should be a simple ticket form, a support email placeholder, or a future portal action.

### Make Status Easy to Understand

Customers should be able to see whether a ticket is new, being reviewed, waiting on customer input, planned for follow-up, or closed.

### Make Communication Respectful

Updates should be specific, useful, and written for business owners as well as technical contacts.
