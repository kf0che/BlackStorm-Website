## The Short Version

We know most people do not read privacy policies from top to bottom. Here are the most important points:

- We collect contact and account information when you contact us, sign up, or use our services.
- We collect monitoring data about the devices you ask us to monitor.
- We use that information to provide monitoring, alerts, dashboards, reports, support, and billing.
- We do not sell your personal information.
- We do not share your monitoring data with advertisers.
- We do not inspect the content of your network traffic.
- You can ask us to access, correct, export, or delete your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us, create an account, request service, or communicate with support, we may collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Company or organization name
- Billing and subscription information
- Login credentials, stored securely
- Support requests, contact form messages, and related communications
- Service preferences and account settings

Payment information may be processed by third-party payment providers. BlackStorm should not store full payment card numbers on its own systems unless explicitly stated in a future attorney-reviewed version of this policy.

### 1.2 Network Monitoring Data

When you use BlackStorm's Network Monitoring Service, we collect data needed to provide monitoring and alerting. This may include:

- IP addresses, hostnames, or device names you add to monitoring
- Device availability status, such as online or offline state
- Response times and latency measurements
- Packet loss or similar performance indicators, where supported
- Alert events, timestamps, recovery events, and incident history
- Device labels, notes, locations, or descriptions you add
- Dashboard and report data generated from monitoring activity

This monitoring data belongs to you. BlackStorm uses it to provide the service, generate alerts, support dashboards, create reports, troubleshoot issues, and maintain the platform.

### 1.3 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or use our online services, we may automatically collect limited technical information, such as:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device and operating system type
- Pages visited
- Date and time of access
- Referring page or general traffic source
- Basic usage information for security, analytics, and troubleshooting

### 1.4 Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

BlackStorm does not intentionally collect:

- The content of your network traffic
- Files, documents, or private data stored on monitored devices
- Social Security numbers
- Government-issued identification numbers
- Health or medical records
- Sensitive financial account numbers
- Personal information about other network users beyond the device and account information needed to provide monitoring

If this changes in the future, this policy should be updated and reviewed before launch.
